Vishdom

I don’t just work in newsrooms; I build them. As a producer, journalist, and newsroom head, I spend my days creating processes that last, shaping stories that matter, and mastering technologies both old and new. For over 13 years, I’ve rolled up my sleeves in every corner of journalism — reporting, editing, production, design-thinking, storytelling — learning by doing and refusing to settle for anything ordinary. Print, digital, broadcast — I’ve collaborated with some of India’s most talented people and companies, creating identities, news pieces, experiences, and stories that stand their ground.

This site isn’t a portfolio in the conventional sense. It’s a space beyond social streams, a workshop where I share ideas, experiments, and reflections. Here, you’ll see the curiosity, the rigor, and the stubbornness that drives my work — a place to explore how stories, systems, and platforms can be built, not just consumed.

I focus on problem-solving, staying consistent, and building with purpose.
