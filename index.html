Inside Out

As a producer, journalist, and newsroom head, I spend my days building processes, crafting stories, and exploring both time-tested and emerging technologies. Over the past 13 years as a multidisciplinary storyteller, I’ve dived into every corner of journalism — production, design-thinking, and storytelling — learning by doing, hands-on. Print, digital, broadcast — I’ve had the privilege of working with some of India’s most talented people and companies, creating identities, news pieces, experiences, and films along the way.

This site isn’t just a portfolio. It’s a space beyond social streams where I can share my thoughts, ideas, and experiments — a place to explore what excites me about storytelling and media today.
