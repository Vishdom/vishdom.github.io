Hi, I’m Vishwanath Patil

I’ve lived the newsroom in all its forms — from reporting on defense and politics to producing cricket fever, from live elections broadcasts to building AI-driven workflows that change how media gets made. I don’t just adapt to media shifts; I build for them. That means creating stories that matter, systems that scale, and platforms that grow. I bring the curiosity of a journalist, the agility of a startup, and the stubbornness of a builder who won’t settle for the ordinary. This space is my workshop. Here, you’ll find the work I’ve done, the stories I’ve shaped, and the experiments I’ve pushed into reality — always chasing better ways to inform, engage, and imagine what’s next.
